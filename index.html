How To Use
NEON AURA AR
Hand tracking · Air drawing · Lightning arcs
📸 Camera access is required. When prompted by your browser, tap Allow to enable your front camera. Without it, hand tracking will not work.
Getting Started
1
Allow Camera
Tap Allow when browser asks for camera permission. Use a well-lit room facing your camera.
2
Raise Your Hands
Hold one or both hands open, facing the camera at arm's length. Keep fingers spread for best tracking.
3
Watch the Magic
Neon arcs and skeleton lines appear instantly. Bring both hands close to each other for lightning effects.
Gestures
🤌
PINCH
Bring thumb + index finger together → shockwave burst + zap sound
🖐️
OPEN HAND
Spread all fingers wide → shows spread % in HUD
✊
FIST
Close fingers together → detected as Fist mode
🙌
BOTH PALMS
Use two hands → lightning arcs + mandala pattern between fingertips
✏️
AIR DRAW
Switch to Draw mode → pinch to paint glowing trails in the air
⚡
CLOSE HANDS
Bring index fingers of both hands near each other → electric sparks fire
Toolbar (Right Side)
⚡
Arc Mode
Default mode — shows lightning arcs and neon skeleton on your hands.
✏️
Air Draw Mode
Pinch your index finger and thumb together to draw glowing strokes in mid-air. Release to stop.
📷
Screenshot
Saves a full composite PNG of your current AR frame — camera + effects + drawing.
⏺
Record Video
Tap once to start recording, tap again to stop and download as .webm video file.
Tips
💡
Best Performance
Use in good lighting. Dark backgrounds work best — your hands pop against the neon effects.
🎨
Change Theme
Use the theme buttons at the bottom — Rainbow, Cyber, Lava, Ocean, Galaxy — to change all arc colors.
